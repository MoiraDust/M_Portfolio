Hello everyone! I am Moira. Well ... In fact I don't know what I should write here to make it more interesting or engaging. I don't consider myself to be wonderfully eloquent or articulate. Probably because I prefer doing it to saying it.

In short, I am a person who is curious about the unknown. This curiosity is what drives me to learn. For example, when I view a great looking page, I would love to find out how it was made and then take the initiative to learn and understand the technology stack used for the page.

I like pretty things and occasionally try to design some pretty pages myself, but so far I've not been particularly happy with the results. I also like to work with image editing software such as Photoshop, which I am not proficient in but I can do some simple image manipulation using Photoshop.

Apart from that, I love music, especially classical and light music. I have saved up money from some part-time jobs to buy an electric piano. Although I don't have a lot of time to study music, I always play a little part of music when I get the chance. I hope that one day in the future I will be able to play a full song and find a street piano to play it on.

Another thing I have to say when it comes to me is that I am an avid fan of ACG (anime, manga, games). I think these works, like books, express the author's perspective on the world very well. Through different works I can experience different lives and think and look at the world from a different perspective.

That's about all I have to say about myself. I hope that through this informal text you will get to know me better.
